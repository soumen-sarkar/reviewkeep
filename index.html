Why Do You Need Reviews?

Want consumers to find you online?
You need to have a high quantity of positive reviews on multiple review sites!

All marketplaces are now digital. That means consumers find you and learn about your business digitally as well. Even if you have a storefront, consumers will identify and learn about you long before they walk in the door. Nothing improves your online presence more than having hundreds or thousands of positive reviews that are readily available to view.


Brand Trust, Credibility, Quality, and Reputation are increased by Reviews

  • 97% of consumers read online reviews for local businesses, with 12% looking for a local business online everyday

  • 85% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations

  • Positive reviews make 73% of consumers trust a local business more

  • 49% of consumers need at least a four-star rating before they choose to use a business
